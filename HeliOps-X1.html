HELIOPS-X1

Decision Dominance from Space to Battle Ground

Problem Statement

Our soldiers operate in rapidly changing environments where comms/GPS is often denied and reliance of perfect networks is uncertain.
Absence of Real Time Situational Awareness Systems during Combat Missions.

Solution

A Drone/UAS with controller and a Mobile Receiver Device with HeliOps-X1 as a AI software equiped as a gear by each Men into Ops!!
The efficiency of rapid decision-making with real-time data allows for accuracy and increased situational awareness.

Capabilities

| 01

Real-Time 2D Mapping

Generate instant 2D maps with drone footage in real time — from broad operational views to detailed terrain models. With HeliOps-X1, operators with UAS and mobile devices access on-demand intelligence across the full range of the UAS, eliminating reliance on larger ISR platforms. Live drone and sensor data deliver tactical clarity, enabling instant situational awareness, route planning, and adaptive decision-making — even in signal-denied environments.

About the tool

Operators on the tactical edge, in signal-denied environments, often use UAS for operational ISR. With HeliOps-X1, operators equipped with UAS and a mobile device can launch and immediately access on-demand intelligence with as much range as the UAS allows. This eliminates dependencies on larger ISR platforms and increased situational awareness.

HeliOps-X1 gives operators near real-time 3D (NRT3D) mapping capabilities in the palm of their hands. Models are available to the users in as little as 2 minutes. Accurate 3D mapping helps to streamline mission planning.

| 03

Line Of Sight

Line of sight tools uncover exposure risks and tactical sightlines that save lives, expose threats, and guide positioning.

Details

Once 3D Modeling of an AOI is achieved, users can utilize HeliOps-X1’s line of sight (LOS) tool to quickly understand vantage points. Whether using LOS to avoid detection on a reconnaissance mission or to specifically plan a diversion, HeliOps-X1 allows the tactical teams to be more creative on the battlefield.

| 05

HLZ Surveying

HeliOps-X1's AI Route Planning tool intelligently suggests the most advantageous path from Point A to Point B. Routes are suggested based on maximum and minimum slope of terrain.

Video
Details

Instead of sending teams forward for reconnaissance, HeliOps-X1 allows users to conduct Helicopter Landing Zone(HLZ) surveys, record grid information, and transfer to air assets via ATAK in real time. HeliOps-X1 considers doctrinal limits and airframe capabilities to suggest HLZ locations. Users can input the required landing zone size and maximum allowable slope, and HeliOps-X1 suggests optimal landing zones on appropriate clearings or rooftops. Designed to evaluate HLZs even in hostile or complex terrain, HLZ surveys with HeliOps-X1 are now faster, safer, and fully digitized.
